About

I'm Alakmar Tinwala (also Al Aqmar), 13 years old, based in Mandsaur, Madhya Pradesh, India. I build production AI systems, ship open source tools, and write books — all while thinking at infrastructure scale.

My flagship product eSAMz AI has grown to 3,000+ users organically since October 2025 without a single rupee in paid advertising. The system runs on a Rust backend, making it one of the more performance-conscious indie AI products from India.

I care deeply about privacy-first design — eSAMz stores zero conversation data by architecture, not just policy. I also believe great products come from moving fast, thinking in systems, and not being precious about pivoting.
